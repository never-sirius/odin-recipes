Garlic Butter

This garlic butter recipe is restaurant-worthy, perfect for spreading on bread or adding flavor to your favorite dishes. It's simple to make with just a few ingredients, but the results are incredible!

Garlic Butter Ingredients

  • 2 sticks of plain, softened butter
  • 1 tablespoon fresh minced garlic
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. In a bowl, mix all the ingredients until they are well-blended.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a serving dish or an airtight container.
  4. Use immediately or store in the refrigerator for up to one week.

How to Use Garlic Butter

This garlic butter is incredibly versatile! Use it to make garlic bread, or spread it on barbecued steaks, pasta, rice, or potatoes. You can also try it in recipes like Lazy Chicken Parmesan Grilled Cheese or Air Fryer Steak Bites.

Storage Instructions

Store the garlic butter in a shallow airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one week. You can also freeze it for up to three months. For easy use, freeze in tablespoon-sized portions using an ice cube tray.
